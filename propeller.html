About Propeller Selection

Propeller selection plays a major role in determining thrust efficiency, stability, vibration levels, and current consumption of a drone. A mismatched propeller can overload motors, reduce flight time, and cause excessive vibration.

This tool helps choose the correct propeller diameter and pitch based on motor KV rating, battery voltage, and drone weight. Proper matching ensures smooth airflow, stable hover, and improved power efficiency.

Correct propeller choice also reduces ESC stress and improves overall flight reliability.
