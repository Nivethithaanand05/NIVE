Battery Helper

About Battery Selection

Battery selection directly affects flight time, safety, and performance of a drone. Choosing an undersized battery can lead to voltage sag, overheating, and mid-air shutdowns.

This tool estimates required battery capacity, discharge C-rating, and safety margin based on current consumption and voltage.

Proper battery sizing improves endurance, prevents damage to electronics, and ensures consistent power delivery during aggressive maneuvers and payload flight.

3S → small drone

4S → medium drone

6S → heavy drone
